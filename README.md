The Computation and Data Science Education (CDSE) initiative helps instructors within the University of Toronto’s Faculty of Arts &amp; Science integrate computational and data-science methods tailored to their disciplines and courses.   
Working alongside the broader Faculty of Arts & Science Teaching & Learning team, we offer a range of pedagogical and technical supports for existing and new courses, including: 
* Consultations on the development of courses, course components and teaching strategies related to computation and data science  
* Development of course materials leveraging our in-house toolkit found at datatools.utoronto.ca - allowing assignment completion and submission using Python or R 
* Development of written resources and guides for instructors, teaching assistants, and/or students
